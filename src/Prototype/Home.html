HealthcareDataViz

Welcome to Healthcare data visualization for UK!

This website helps personnel in General Practices to understand and discover prescription costs. It provides intuitive way to visualize the data. A user can search a GP to view it's profiles or add it to a focus group for analysing the prescription costs further. Prescription costs can be explored at BNF Chapters level (together) or at a specific drug group level.

The organization and spending api is provided by "OpenPrescribing.net, EBM DataLab, University of Oxford, 2017".
